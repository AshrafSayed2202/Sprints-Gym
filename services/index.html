TRAINING & CLASSES

TRAINING

Personal Training

You can think of personal training like our secret sauce, it’s the fastest and safest way to get seriously awesome results. Our experienced trainers develop fun (yes, fun!) individualized fitness programs that incorporate cutting-edge exercises and nutrition to help you crush your fitness goals.




Nutrition

Proper nutrition fuels your workouts and helps you get the most out of your time at the club. That’s why our trainers work with you to develop customized nutrition plans that focus on your specific needs, tastes, and dietary restrictions.

Build a true fight with boxing and kickboxing classes. We honor the fighting traditions by rigorously adapting them into hard-hitting martial arts classes that will bring out your inner warrior.



ATHLETIC TRAINING

Our calorie-torching HIIT workouts will leave you breathless and feeling burn in all your muscles.

Pool workouts target every muscle group in a single class. Low-impact yet high-intensity, our swimming classes combine classic strokes with innovative training tools, making one-of-a-kind, full-body workouts

Cardio training meets inspired playlists, with a variety of drills, intervals and team challenges at our signature cycling classes. Climb, sprint and let the energy move you.
